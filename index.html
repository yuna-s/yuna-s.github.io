🌷 About me

Hi, I'm Yuna — nice to meet you! I'm a curious person who enjoys writing, making things, and finding beauty in small everyday moments.

This blog is my little corner of the internet where I share thoughts on tech, books, creative hobbies, and whatever is currently on my mind. No strict schedule — just genuine writing when I have something to say.

When I'm not at a keyboard, you might find me browsing bookstores, trying new cafes, or getting lost in a new hobby. ☕
